About Noir Blooms

Noir Blooms was born from a love of the mysterious, the gothic, and the natural beauty of the Black Dahlia flower. We aim to blend dark elegance with timeless artistry, offering unique products for those who embrace individuality and beauty beyond the ordinary.

Our Mission

To provide a curated selection of gothic-inspired fashion, art, beauty, and accessories that celebrate the beauty of darkness and personal expression.

The Black Dahlia Flower

Black Dahlia is a gorgeous and mysterious flower with dark, plush colors ranging from deep burgundy to near black. The flower is not black but rather a very dark red or purple color. Symbolically, the black dahlia typically represents a splash of elegance, power, and a splash of mystery or intrigue. It's a designer favorite when used in dramatic flower arrangements and gothic-style gardens, sought after for both its beauty and bold, nonconformist aesthetic.
